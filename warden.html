How to play

You are the keep's mounted warden. Drag to ride; charging raiders that reach the gate steal a heart, so meet them in the field. Tap the stance button to switch weapon: Bow shoots at range on its own, while the Lance cuts down anyone you ride through (no arrows — you must dive in). The enemy adapts to whatever you favour: lean on the bow and they send skirmishers, who dodge most arrows but fall to the lance; lean on the lance and they send pikemen, who brace against a charge but are soft to arrows. Shieldbearers block both weapons from the front — flank them. Brutes just soak hits. Every repelled raider drops gold. Between bands, spend it in the Muster: raise Footmen who roam and fight, build Watchtowers that guard the keep, invest in a Granary for more gold each band, or sharpen the warden — then march. Survive as long as you can.

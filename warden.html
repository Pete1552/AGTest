How to play

You are the keep's mounted warden. Hold and drag anywhere — your horse rides toward your finger, and your archer looses arrows at the nearest raider all by herself. Raiders march in slow bands toward the gate; any that reach it steal a heart and slip away. Ride out to meet them, circle behind stragglers, and keep the keep. From the fifth band on, your archer nocks extra arrows. Survive as many bands as you can.

How to play

You are the keep's mounted warden. Hold and drag anywhere — your horse rides toward your finger, and your archer looses arrows on her own. Raiders march in bands toward the gate and charge the last stretch, so waiting at the walls loses; ride out to meet them. Each raider wants a different answer: runners are fast and must be caught early, shieldbearers block arrows from the keep-side so you must flank them, and brutes soak several hits. After every band you draft one of three boons to build up your warden. Any raider who reaches the gate steals a heart; survive as many bands as you can.

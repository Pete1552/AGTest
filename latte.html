How to play

Each round shows a faint design on the cup. Drag to pour the milk — hold still to let it bloom into a puddle, move slowly for a wide stream or quickly for a thin one. Fill the shape without spilling outside it. Undo or Clear to adjust, then Done to score. Five pours; highest total wins.

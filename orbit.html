How to play

Drop celestial bodies into the well — when two of the same kind touch, they fuse into the next, bigger one: spark, mote, pebble, comet, moon, planet, ringed world, giant, star… and finally a sun. Two suns cancel out entirely. Plan your drops so the pile doesn't rise past the line. Bigger fusions score more. On a phone, tilting the device gently rolls the pile.

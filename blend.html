How to play

Ringed tiles are locked colour anchors. Tap any movable tile to pick it up, then tap another to swap their colours. Rebuild the smooth colour gradient so every tile flows into the next — in as few swaps as you can. Harder levels use bigger boards, closer hues, and on Expert the anchors are scattered through the grid like crossword clues instead of sitting in the corners.

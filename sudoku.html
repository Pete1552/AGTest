How to play

Fill the grid so every row, column and 3×3 box contains 1-9 exactly once. Tap a cell, then tap a number — clashes show in red. Long-press a number (or turn on ✎ notes) to pencil small candidate marks into a cell; placing a real number tidies that note out of its row, column and box. On a keyboard use Shift+number for notes and N to toggle notes mode. Every puzzle is generated with exactly one solution. Fastest solve wins, per difficulty.

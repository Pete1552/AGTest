How to play

Raise a stone tower over the water. Drag each stone where you want it, rotate it if it helps, and drop it — real gravity decides what stands. You get 24 stones; anything that topples into the water is gone for good. Your score is the height of whatever is still standing at the end. Build wide for safety, or narrow and brave for the leaderboard.

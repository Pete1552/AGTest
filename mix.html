How to play

Match the target colour by blending paint. Tap the six paints to add drops — your mix is the average of every drop. You get a limited number of drops each round, so find the right ratio. Undo or Clear to adjust, then Check to score. Later rounds get trickier. Six rounds; highest total wins.

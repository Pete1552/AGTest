How to play

Build a tiny village, one tile at a time. Tap a spot to preview your points, tap again to build. Each building earns from what's already beside it — groves love groves, fields love fields and ponds, cottages want greenery and a well, wells serve cottages, and chapels count every cottage within two tiles (but sulk near another chapel). One stash slot lets you save a tile for later. Place all 30 tiles for your final score.

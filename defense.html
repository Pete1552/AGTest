How to play

Walkers march along the path; flyers cut straight over the board ignoring it. Pick a tower below, then tap an empty tile to build (they auto-fire at enemies in range) — tap a tower to upgrade it. Cannons hit walkers hard but flyers softly; Flak towers do the opposite. Roadblocks drop directly on the path and slow any walker passing through for a few seconds before crumbling — great for holding enemies in a tower's range — but flyers ignore the path entirely, so roadblocks can't touch them. Kills earn coins. Each enemy that reaches the base costs a life — lose them all and it's over. Survive as many waves as you can.

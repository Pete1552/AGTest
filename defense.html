How to play

Enemies march along the path toward your base. Tap an empty tile to build a tower (they auto-fire at enemies in range). Tap a tower to upgrade its range and damage. Kills earn coins. Each enemy that reaches the base costs a life — lose all your lives and it's over. Survive as many waves as you can.

No ads. No pop-ups. No nags — ever.

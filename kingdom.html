How to play

Grow your kingdom by claiming tiles next to your territory. Every tile shows a defense number — the strength of whoever is holding it. Scout (power 1) is cheap but leaves a weak garrison; Warrior (power 4) is expensive but breaks tough tiles and holds the line; Builder (power 2) leaves a modest garrison and turns the tile into a farm, earning gold and wood every turn. To take an enemy tile, your unit's power must beat its defense number. The rival kingdom grows stronger the longer it expands, and it will strike back at your weakly-held border tiles — so decide what's worth defending. Hold out and stronger recruits join you too: Champion (power 6) unlocks at turn 10, General (power 9) at turn 20, so your ceiling keeps pace with theirs. The game ends once every tile is spoken for; the more of the board you hold, the better your score.

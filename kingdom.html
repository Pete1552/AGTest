How to play

Grow your kingdom one tile at a time. Pick a unit — Scout, Warrior, or Builder — then tap any tile next to your territory to claim it, paying that unit's gold and wood cost. Every claim earns back gold and wood, so keep expanding. The rival kingdom claims a tile of its own after each of your moves. The game ends once every tile is spoken for — the more of the board you hold, the better your score.
